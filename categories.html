Available Services

Orthodontics

Treatment for misaligned teeth, underbites, overbites and crossbites using braces, retainers and other specialized treatments.

Endodontics

Deals with the sensitive interior of your tooth when it is damaged, infected, or diseased. Treatments include using root canal therapy.

Periodontics

Prevents and treats coniditons and diseases that affect the structures that support your teeth.

Prosthodontics

Replace and restore your damaged or missing teeth using designed artificial replacements. Treatments include installing bridges, dental implants and dentures.

Cosmetic dentistry

Improves your smile and self-confidence through teeth whitening or bleaching. The latter reduces stains and discolouration to give you a brighter smile.

Oral & maxillofacial surgery

Wisdom tooth extraction, as they don't get enough room to erupt properly so they can cause infection and damage other teeth and oral bone.

Pediatric dentistry

Cater to dental needs and oral health of children, ranging from infants to teens. Routine services include teeth cleaning and cavity filling as well as monitoring.

Dental bonding

It is a technique used to correct imperfections with your teeth to give you a better looking smile. It is used to repair chipped, decayed and cracked teeth.

General dentistry

Monitoring the overall health of your teeth and oral hygiene. Offers restorative dentistry including implants, bridges, tooth decay treatments and cleaning.
