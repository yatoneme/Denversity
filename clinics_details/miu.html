Vision and Mission

Vision

The Dental Clinics Complex (DCC) at Misr International University (MIU) aims to be a well-recognized and an internationally accredited dental care institution.

Mission

The Dental Clinics Complex (DCC) at Misr International University (MIU) is an educational healthcare institution that participates in community service through providing free of charge high quality dental healthcare services with strict commitment to infection control and safety guidelines. It also provides high quality dental education, clinical training on the latest trends and technologies and facilitates research activities for staff members, postgraduate students, interns and students to equip them with the skills needed to compete in the market.

Patient Safety and Infection Prevention & Control

Our health care system focuses on patient safety in order to prevent harm Everyone is involved in identifying risk and opportunities to make care safer. In our system every patient has access to high-quality care. Patient’s values, preferences, and expressed needs are at the center of any decision made. Coordination and integration of care, communication and emotional support are factored into health care plans and actions.

All the measures of “a safety culture” are adopted and implemented:

  1. All workers (including front line staff, dentists, physicians, and administrators) accept responsibility for their own safety, their co-workers, patients, and visitors’ safety.
  2. Safety has priority over other operational goals.
  3. Identification, communication, and resolution of safety issues are encouraged and rewarded.
  4. There are provisions for learning from accidents.
  5. Appropriate resources, structure, and accountability are allocated to maintain effective safety systems.

Infection Prevention and Control

A comprehensive Infection Prevention and Control (IPC) program has been developed implemented and monitored through a well-organized structure comprising qualified, dedicated technical staff with defined responsibilities, scope and functions. The program adopts the current evidence based guidelines and the most up-to date practice standards to promote safe high quality dental health care.
